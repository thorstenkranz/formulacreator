Samples

This page demonstrates how formula creator can be used to render formulas. The base-url for formula rendering is 

http://formulacreator.appengine.com/formula/

All instructions for rendering are put into the HTTP-Querystring. Simply attach a '?' to the url and some 'key=value'-pairs. Modern browsers even take care of correct url-encoding for you.

If you plan to use it from other environments, e.g., your own website, you must take care of it on your own - or use our editor!
